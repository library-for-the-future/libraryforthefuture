library/for/the/future

What does a radical feminist library look like today? Which books are feminists turning to for thought-partnership? How might a collection of texts help us imagine new feminist futures?

library/for/the/future is a network of physical and digital spaces designed to support feminist thoughtpartnershipand resource-sharing. We collaborate with artists to generate feminist library collections around a central question or theme.

Each collection gathers books that have informed readers’ relationships to feminism. The content, location, and physical form of every library collection is unique; in this sense, it is a resource for the future and a record of a particular kind of thinking at a particular time and place. library/for/the/future takes its shape from the dialogue between these collections.
